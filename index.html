What Is A Qr Code For?

Qr code stands for quick response code, and it is a technology with a wide range of use cases, such as storing URLs, contact information, or even payment information. Basically Qr codes help you represent any piece of information be it a website url, vcard, or even a phone number as a code that can be scanned by a computer and then that piece of information will be transfered to that computer.

What You Can Do With A Qr Code

With a Qr code you can share links to any website and once a person scans your qr code, they will be taken to that website
You can create a qr code for your whatsapp account, so that when a lerson scans this code they will be directed to your whatsapp DM
You can also create a Qr code for sharing your vcard, so that once this code is scanned the scanner will have your virtual card file
